## Switch between languages
 
After you install the Microsoft Office 2010 English Language Pack, you can switch between languages in your Office applications. To do this, follow these steps:

- Open an Office application, such as Word or Excel.
- Click File and then click Options.
- In the Options dialog box, click Language.
- Under Choose Editing Languages, select the language that you want to use and then click Set as Default.
- Click OK to save your changes.

You may need to restart your Office application for the changes to take effect.

- Follow the instructions on the screen to complete the uninstallation.
